ğŸ’° CORE CONCEPT: POINT-BASED CURRENCY SYSTEM
Points function like money in an RPG:
  - EARNED from days alive + kills made
  - SPENT on upgrades (each costs points)
  - DEDUCTED from balance when purchased
  - Tracked via spentPoints (running total of spent)
  - Budget = totalPoints - spentPoints

ğŸ¯ HOW POINT SPENDING WORKS

1. Mob spawns with point budget (based on day + kills)
2. Every tick (1/20th second):
   a. Check: spentPoints < totalPoints?
   b. Collect all affordable upgrades
   c. SMART SAVING: 50% chance to skip cheap upgrades (<5 pts)
      when expensive ones (>=10 pts) are available
   d. Choose one randomly from filtered list
   e. Apply upgrade immediately
   f. Deduct cost: spentPoints += upgradeCost
   g. Save to PowerProfile NBT
   h. Visual effects (particles, equipment appears)
3. Repeat until spentPoints >= totalPoints (fully upgraded)
4. New kills/days â†’ More points â†’ More upgrades!

SMART SAVING EXAMPLE:
  Available: Health Boost (2 pts), Horde Summon (10 pts)
  50% chance: Skip Health Boost, save for Horde Summon
  50% chance: Buy either upgrade
  Result: Mob sometimes saves for expensive upgrades!
